Privacy Policy

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

1.1 Express Couriers & Co (2206825 Alberta Ltd.) is committed to safeguarding the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy delineates our practices regarding the collection, storage, processing, transfer, sharing, use of personal information and our use of cookies and similar technologies.

1.3 This policy applies comprehensively to all interactions with our Service.

1.4 By accessing or using our Service, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review it thoroughly to understand how your Personal Information is handled.

2. Summary of Our Privacy Practices

2.1 We collect Personal Information directly from you, as well as automatically through technical means like IP addresses, browser information, and cookies to enhance user experience and Service functionality.

2.2 Personal Information can be gathered from subscribers, our own operated websites, and third parties, used for Service operation, analytics, content personalization, and lawful marketing activities.

2.3 We do not disclose Personal Information to third parties outside of what is permitted under this policy or needed for Service provision. Information you choose to make public on our platforms may be accessible to others.

2.4 Your Personal Information is stored on secure servers with controlled access. International data transfers are conducted with appropriate legal safeguards.

2.5 You have rights concerning your Personal Information, including access, correction, deletion, and objection. Contact us at admin@expresscouriers.co to exercise these rights.

2.6 This summary provides an overview; the detailed terms below offer more comprehensive information.

3.1 We collect Personal Information when you engage with us through forms, communications via phone, email, or other means. This includes registration for Service use, participation in social media functions, or entering promotions.

3.2 Failure to provide Personal Information may prevent us from providing certain aspects of the Service or responding to your requests.

3.3 We may receive Personal Information about you from subscribers, other websites we manage, or from third parties under contractual obligations to protect such data.

3.6 We may combine or anonymize collected data to provide a personalized service or for analysis, ensuring individual identification is not possible where data is anonymized.
