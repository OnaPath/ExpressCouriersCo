RE-ATTEMPT CHARGE (WRONG ADDRESS / CLOSED)

If Express Couriers & Co cannot deliver a delivery due to an incorrect address, we will do our utmost to locate the correct destination. After verification from the customer, we will attempt delivery again. If delivery fails because the recipient's location is closed, we will contact the customer for further guidance or proceed according to the 'if closed' instructions given at the time of order. Alternatively, customers can choose to have the delivery returned. Each additional delivery attempt will incur a fee matching the original delivery charge, and returns will cost at least as much as the initial delivery.

If Express Couriers & Co cannot complete a delivery pickup, the shipper will be notified, and a no-trip charge equivalent to the base rate of the delivery will be applied.

WAITING TIME (INCLUDING LOAD AND UNLOAD)
