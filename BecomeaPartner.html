Express Couriers & Co is a boutique on-demand delivery service located in Alberta. We understand the logistics and tech involved in offering on-demand delivery isn't available to small to medium sized businesses. We're here to help.

We've built out a number of integrations with marketplaces, Point of Sale systems, and Payment Processors to streamline the delivery experience for your business.

We accommadate SellSafe and ProServe certified couriers depending on your businesses needs. For deliveries that require ID checks our couriers are trained and implement a strict government issued ID checking policy including a check in place in the courier app that requires an ID check prior to handoff to make sure your business and our couriers are covered.
