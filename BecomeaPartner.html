Become a Partner

The Personal and Professional Delivery Network

Express Couriers & Co is a boutique on-demand delivery service located in Alberta. We understand the logistics and tech involved in offering on-demand delivery isn't available to small to medium sized businesses. We're here to help.

We've built out a number of integrations with marketplaces, Point of Sale systems, and Payment Processors to streamline the delivery experience for your business.

We accommadate SellSafe and ProServe certified couriers depending on your businesses needs. For deliveries that require ID checks our couriers are trained and implement a strict government issued ID checking policy including a check in place in the courier app that requires an ID check prior to handoff to make sure your business and our couriers are covered.

We are looking to expand into all retail industries in Alberta, preferred partners may receive discounts on point a to point b hotshot delivery solutions.

If you're curious about trying the delivery service simply visit the delivery page in your city and request a delivery. A nearby courier will be dispatched for delivery and the customer will receive status updates on their delivery.

Join our network of local business partners and expand your delivery capabilities with Express Couriers & Co.

Benefits of being a Preferred Partner

  • Volume Discounts: Partners with regular delivery needs receive reduced rates on every delivery
  • Priority Dispatch: Your deliveries get priority matching with our nearest available courier
  • Dedicated Support: Direct access to our support team for quick issue resolution
  • Custom Integration: We'll help set up delivery integration with your existing systems
  • Flexible Billing: Monthly invoicing options available for preferred partners
  • Business Account Login: Simply login to your account for saved details on pickup locations for streamlined delivery requests
