About Express Couriers & Co

Your Local Hotshot Delivery Solution

Express Couriers & Co specializes in same-city hotshot delivery services across Alberta. When you need something delivered quickly within your city, our professional couriers are ready to help with immediate pickup and delivery.

What We Do

We provide rapid point-to-point delivery within city limits. Whether you need documents rushed across town or a package delivered across the city, our network of local couriers ensures your items arrive at their destination quickly and safely.

Our Commitment

At Express Couriers & Co, we focus on speed and reliability for local deliveries. Our technology matches you with the nearest available courier, getting your items picked up and delivered as quickly as possible within your city.

How it Works

Express Couriers & Co is a network of friendly, professional independent contractors operating personal vehicles with a valid Class 5 license.

Contractors are responsible for ensuring their insurance coverage is up to date and their vehicle is in good working order.

Express Couriers & Co operates solely in the province of Alberta offering on-demand delivery within city limits of the locations we operate in to the general public and small businesses.
